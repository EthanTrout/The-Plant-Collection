Plants

What plants do we sell

The plants we sell and the plants that Fiona, the owner, chooses from her own personal collection. this could range from huge monsteras to cuttings from a string of hearts plant

Most of the plants that we stock are going to be easy to look after and if not, come in and you can always ask for care tips from Fiona

Plant care tips

  • Watering your plants obviously! Different plants need diferent amounts of water. They need less in winter and more in summer
  • Fertiliser can be used with more mature plants but less is usually more
  • Find the right spot in your home for your plant, some like more light and some like less

If you feel a little unconfident about potting and planting, Fiona can offer a wealth of advice and the odd masterclass, as well as stocking everything you need from peat to pruning shears.

Who are we?

At The Plant Collection we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it's an experience that awakens the senses and fosters connections. Our commitment to quality starts with sourcing the finest, ethically grown beans from around the world. We prioritize relationships with sustainable coffee producers who share our values, ensuring that every sip tells a story of craftsmanship and care.

Step into our cozy and inviting space filled with plants, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee welcomes you. Our skilled baristas, fuelled by a passion for the craft, meticulously prepare each cup, utilizing precision and expertise to extract the rich flavours and unique characteristics of our carefully chosen beans.

Plants

its called the plant collection for a reason!

We stock a large range of house plants, from little starter plants to much larger established plants! If you feel a little unconfident about potting and planting, We can offer a wealth of advice and the occasional masterclass, as well as stocking everything you need from soils to pruning shears.

We offer full aftercare on all plants purchased from ourselves as well, just incase it all goes wrong once you get your new plant home

Fantastic coffee and cakes. The shop staff were fantastic. Attentive, funny, and clearly love running the place. Plants everywhere, which is novel, and pleasant. 10/10 for this place. Vegetarian options: They are very allergen friendly with the cakes etc. Parking: Parking is not obvious, but is to the rear of the the property. Access via side of the building. Kid-friendliness: Menu and the owners couldn't do enough for the kids.

Joanna

Love this coffee shop. We have visited regularly and each time have had amazing coffee and cake. We've purchased a few plants from here and the staff are very knowledgeable about each of them and explained how to care for them at home. It's great to see they are supporting other small businesses too, selling a variety of products from businesses local to Crossgates. We will be back very soon!
